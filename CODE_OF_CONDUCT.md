# Code of Conduct

This document outlines the expected standards of behavior for all participants, how to report violations, and how enforcement decisions are made.

Cohesix is an open source project dedicated to building secure, high-performance, and community-driven software. We expect all contributors to uphold the highest standards of respectful, inclusive, and constructive behavior.

## Expected Behavior

All participants in the Cohesix project are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Foster an inclusive environment, welcoming diverse perspectives and experiences.
- Assume good intent and seek clarification before making judgments.
- Provide constructive feedback and collaborate in good faith.
- Act with integrity, especially regarding security and project integrity.

## Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.
- Personal attacks, threats, or offensive comments.
- Sustained disruption of conversations or development processes.
- Misrepresentation of the project or unauthorized disclosure of sensitive information.

## Reporting Guidelines

To report violations or concerns:

- Email the project lead at **conduct@cohesix.org**
- Include a clear description of the issue, with supporting evidence where appropriate.
- Reports will be handled confidentially and professionally.

## Enforcement

The project maintainers reserve the right to:

- Remove comments or contributions that violate this policy.
- Issue warnings, temporary bans, or permanent exclusion from the project.
- Refer incidents to external authorities when required by law or safety concerns.

All decisions will be guided by fairness, transparency, and the protection of our community.

---

Thank you for contributing to Cohesix and helping create a safe, innovative, and collaborative environment.
