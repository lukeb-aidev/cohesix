Possible causes preventing Cohesix from reaching userland:
- Data abort inside `core::unicode::printable::is_printable` at `0xffffff8040012648`.
- Pointer corruption before namespace application.
- Syscall stubs in `sys.rs` returning `-1` for open/read/exec.
- `/bin/init` missing from the staged filesystem.
- Plan9 namespace not bound due to failed `ensure_plan9_ns`.
- Kernel built in debug mode instead of release.
- Heap allocator writing beyond `__heap_end`.
- Role files under `setup/roles/` missing or not staged.
